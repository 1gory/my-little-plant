# Privacy Policy for My Little Plant

**Last updated: May 28, 2026**

## Data Collection
**This extension does not collect any user data.**

## Information Storage
- Your plant's game state (chosen seed and pot, growth, water and health
  levels, and timestamps) is stored locally in your browser only, via
  `chrome.storage`
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics, tracking, or monitoring is performed
- The extension makes no network requests of any kind

## Data Usage
The stored game state is used solely to keep your plant growing between
sessions and to advance the simulation while the popup is closed. This data
never leaves your device.
